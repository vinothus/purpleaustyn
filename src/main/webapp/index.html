About Us

Purple Austyn Technologies was started In 2018. company envisioned a different kind of vision– one that allowed them, and their employees, to get more out of life and make a better impact on others.

We believe consulting is a team sport, which is why we don’t just work for you – we work with you to uncover new ideas that optimize your processes, elevate your technology, and help you compete in a digital world.

Ways We Give

Our core value of “ignite passion for the greater good” is one we love to carry out on both the national and local level. Our individual teams are also highly committed to several local charitable causes and host team volunteer activities several times a year to serve food, build and repair houses, clean up neighborhoods, and work directly with others in need of support.

Our Leadership

Our Culture

We set out to build the best culture in the consulting industry — one that cares about the work we do and the people we work with. A culture that celebrates individuals and creates lifelong friendships.

This isn’t the first time others have noticed we invest heavily in our culture. Over the years, we’ve been honored for our commitment to employees, clients and communities. Most recently we were recognized as one of Fast Company’s 100 Best Workplaces for Innovators.

We serve our clients by providing industry consulting expertise that can solve their biggest business challenges.
